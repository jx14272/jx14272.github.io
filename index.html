Winter is Coming

find out what the 6 winter solar terms are

LiDong - 立冬 XiaoXue - 小雪 DaXue - 大雪 DongZhi - 冬至 XiaoHan - 小寒 DaHan - 大寒 Notes

LiDong - 立冬

  • Celestial Longtitude 1 : 225°
  • English Translation: Beginning of Winter
  • Water will start to freeze, Soil harderns. Animals on land went start prepares themselves for winter, time to eat more seafood. ( 立冬之日水始冰，又五日地始冻，又五日雉入大水为蜃)
  • Marks the beginning of Winter

XiaoXue - 小雪

  • Celestial Longtitude: 240°
  • English Translation: Light Snow
  • There will be little to none rains, since snows will be taking over. Air current will be changing, the convection formed will cause strong winds. Hence, the signs of winter is clear.( 小雪之日虹藏不见，又五日天气上腾地气下降，又五日闭塞而成冬)
  • Snow starts to fall in some places

DaXue - 大雪

  • Celestial Longtitude: 255°
  • English Translation: Major Snow
  • On the day, even the flying squirrels which is said to be one of the animals that can withstand extreme low temperatures, goes into hibernation. However it is about to reach the coldeset weather, and after the tipping point, there will be life again in the nature. Tigers will go into their mating season, and some strong plants will start to grow.( 大雪之日鹖旦不鸣，又五日虎始交，又五日荔挺生)
  • All places in China should start to snow

DongZhi - 冬至

  • Celestial Longtitude: 270°
  • English Translation: Winter Solstice
  • Earthworm are still trapped inside the frozen lands, but things will get better. The longest night has passed. Some deers and mooses start to grow new antlers. The small creeks in the mountain will start to defrst and begin flowing again. ( 冬至之日蚯蚓结，又五日麋角解，又五日水泉动)
  • Winter Solstice in North Hemisphere is the day that has the shortest day light, and hence, longest night

XiaoHan - 小寒

  • Celestial Longtitude: 285°
  • English Translation: Lesser Cold
  • Some birds start to migrate back to north. The ones that does not migrate and stayed in the north has already started to build their new nest for the coming year. Sensing that winter is start to fade, roosters starts to crow again in the morning. ( 小寒之日雁北乡，又五日鹊始巢，又五日雉始雊)
  • Despite what the animals are doing, the weather is still cold. The day is longer than night now, but the sun is still not providing sufficient heat yet. It is still highly possible to have the coldest day in the year in the next couple weeks.

DaHan - 大寒

  • Celestial Longtitude: 300°
  • English Translation: Great Cold
  • Now is the time to breed small chickens so that by spring, they will start growing. This is also probably near the coldest day of the year, so predators like eagles will be very fierce and try to find anything they can to eat to just live pass the last few days of winter. Due to coldness, still waters like lakes will be frozen all the way through instead of just the top layers. ( 大寒之日鸡使乳，又五日鹫鸟厉疾，又五日水泽腹坚)
  • As said before the coldest day is possibly around this day. But after that Spring should be approaching
